Locations

Amalfi Coast

Amalfi Coast is an Island in Italy. It is known for its beauty and a place for tourists come from all around the world. I recently went to this beautiful place last summer and had a great time.

Tottenham Hotspur Stadium

Tottenham Hotpsurs is a soccer team that are located in London. They have a rich history in winning trophies, however, they have been in a long drought for many years now. It is a wonderful stadium where fans go to support their teams every week!

Madrid Spain

Madrid, the capital of spain. Truly a beautiful place to visit. They have a very prestigious soccer team. They have many trophies in their trophy cabniet. They are known for their gelatos and seafood.
